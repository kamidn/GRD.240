About Us

Founded in 1978, Mouse College is a private institution serving over 1000 students.

MC is located in Computer City, USA on a beautiful 300 kilo byte virtual campus. The college provides degrees in a variety of fields, including, science, technology, arts, and language. In 2000, the college was named one of the best expensive schools in the nation. Click here for a virtual campus tour.
